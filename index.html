    KARATE IS A WAY OF LIFE

    Kishinkai means body and mind. Karate is the perfect way to train the body in ways you never thought possible and to develop the mind to overcome immense personal challenges. Karate takes a lot of dedication and like anything in life, the more effort you put in yields even greater rewards. Under the leadership of Andrew Genery Sensei 8th Dan, with his decades of experience and having received tutelage of some of the greatest Japanese Wadoka such as Suzuki Tatsuo, Shiomitsu Masafumi and Sugasawa Fumio but to name a few, at Kishinkai Wado-Ryu you are in good hands.
